First One for AllDeceased All for One's Brother
Zen's younger twin brother, whom he would name Yoichi (meaning "bestow" and "one"), was the very first thing given to him in life. When they were born, Zen was forced to survive alongside Yoichi in an era of chaos caused by the uprising of Meta Abilities. The two brothers were different to the point of being polar opposites; Zen was born with a powerful Meta Ability, All For One, that could steal other abilities. At the same time, he was also ruthless and used the Quirks he took to kill people. Yoichi was weak and frail, due to his brother taking most of the nutrients from their mother prior to their birth, and was presumed to be Quirkless. However, Yoichi had a strong sense of justice and would try to stop his older brother from harming others, but Zen only kicked Yoichi for standing in his way.
Second GearShiftDeceased Toshitsugu Kudo
Kudo lived in the harshest era of Meta Abilities, when All For One's powers were reaching its peak with his control spreading unchecked. Kudo had foreseen the terrible future All For One will bring upon as he expanded his influence to society. To counteract this, he gathered allies, and formed a resistance to oppose All For One and his new world order. While he had no direct confrontation with this resistance
Third Fa-JinDeceased Bruce Lee😂
When he was alive during the earliest era when Quirks emerged, Bruce became a battle-hardened veteran in an effort to survive the crumbling society. Like Kudo, he has a serious demeanor with apathetic traits due to his experience in history's most chaotic era. He is also a strong believer in viewing the world as nothing more than black and white, as he have believed every time there is victory, it means life, and every time there is defeat, it means death.
Fourth Danger SenseDeceased Hikage Shinomori
Hikage was a valiant and noble man who was determined to defeat All For One. However, he was well aware of his limits and understood he couldn't defeat All For One by himself, so he spent his life training himself and strengthening One For All to be passed onto the next user. Hikage was described by Daigoro Banjo as a "weirdo" who preferred to spend his time in solitude. It is implied that this was due to his Quirk constantly putting him on high alert.
Fifth BlackwhipDeceased Diagoro Loud, passionate, and built like a wall. Banjo's Blackwhip could lash, bind, and launch him across cityscapes. He was the first wielder to speak directly to Midoriya through the vestiges — and the first to believe in him when Izuku could barely believe in himself. "I'm rooting for you, kid."
Sixth Smokescreen Deceased En · "The Quiet One" A man of few words and thick smoke. En could blanket entire battlefields in an instant, turning clarity into chaos for his enemies. He was more ghost than hero — present in the fog, gone before you looked twice. Even his vestige barely speaks. The silence says enough.
Seventh Fa Jin + Float Deceased Nana Shimura She gave up everything — even her son — to protect a world she knew she might not live to see saved. Gran Torino's partner. All Might's master. The woman whose sacrifice echoes loudest in Deku's heart. She smiled at the end. That alone should tell you everything about her character.
Eighth One For All (Peak) Retired Toshinori Yagi · All Might The Symbol of Peace. The man who carried the world's hope on his shoulders for decades — and never once let it drop. Hollowed out by battle, his embers nearly burned out, he passed OFA to a quirkless boy and whispered the words that changed everything: "You can become a hero."
Ninth All Vestiges Unlocked Active Izuku Midoriya · Deku Born quirkless. Mocked, dismissed, told to give up his dream before it even started. Then one man saw what others couldn't — and a boy became a vessel for every hope, every scar, every sacrifice of eight generations. He doesn't just carry OFA. He carries all of them. This is his story.
